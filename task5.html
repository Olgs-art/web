For tourists

The eleven-decked comfortable ship accommodates 593 cabins of various categories, from interior cabins to royal suites with private decks. The ship's crew provides passengers with top-notch all-inclusive services around the clock..

During the cruise, guests will enjoy a rich entertainment program, including three restaurants, five bars, a two-story theater, a cinema, a swimming pool, a spa center, children's clubs, play areas, a variety of excursions in the ports of call, and much more.

The Aurora cruise collection is designed for every passenger who values travel comfort and the relaxed freedom of a cruise vacation. In addition to the classic 7-day trips from Sochi to Turkey, the route palette now includes a variety of 3- to 7-day winter holiday cruises, as well as two-week sea cruises to Egypt.
